Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Inventory Scanner ("the app") is a Progressive Web App for personal and business inventory management. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

The app collects only the data you explicitly enter or that is necessary for the app to function:

  • Barcode numbers you scan or type
  • Product descriptions, quantities, units, and prices you enter or that are fetched from public databases
  • Timestamps of stock updates
  • Google OAuth access tokens — used temporarily in memory to authorise writes to your Google Sheet; never stored on disk or transmitted to any server other than Google
  • Your Google Cloud Client ID — stored in your browser's localStorage on your own device only

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not run any backend servers — there is no database, no accounts system, and no analytics
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not share any data with third parties for advertising or marketing

3. Where Your Data Lives

All inventory data is written directly to your own Google Sheet in your own Google Drive account using the Google Sheets API. The app developer has no access to your spreadsheet. Only you (and anyone you choose to share the sheet with) can see your inventory data.

4. Third-Party Services

The app makes requests to the following external services:

  • Google Sheets API — to read and write your inventory data. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Identity Services (OAuth 2.0) — to authenticate you with your Google account. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Open Food Facts API (openfoodfacts.org) — to look up product names and units from barcodes. Only the barcode number is sent. Governed by the Open Food Facts Privacy Policy.
  • Open Food Facts Prices API (prices.openfoodfacts.org) — to look up estimated prices from barcodes. Only the barcode number is sent.

5. Local Storage

The following data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage (on your device only):

  • Your Google Cloud Client ID
  • Your Google Sheet URL and spreadsheet ID
  • Any offline scan queue (pending syncs)
  • Low stock alert thresholds per barcode

This data never leaves your device except as part of API calls to Google. You can clear it at any time via the "Reset & Reconnect" button in the app's Settings, or by clearing your browser's site data.

6. Camera Access

The app requests access to your device camera solely to scan barcodes. Camera data is processed entirely on your device by the browser's BarcodeDetector API and is never transmitted anywhere.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date above will be updated. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact
